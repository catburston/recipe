Recipe: Boiled Chocolate Cake

Adapted from my mother's recipe

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 125 g butter
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups self-raising flour, sifted

Icing

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 150g sour cream

Method

Cake

Place first five ingredients into a large saucepan. Bring to the boil and simmer for 3 minutes.

Remove from heat and allow to cool for at least half an hour.

Add eggs and flour, and stir well.

Pour into a greased 20 cm cake tin. Bake at 180Â°C for 50 minutes.

Icing

Melt chocolate slowly in a double boiler.

When almost melted, remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.

Slowly stir in sour cream until fully combined.

Spread evenly across the top and sides of cooled cake.
